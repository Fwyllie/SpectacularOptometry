Meet Our Team!

It all started with a dream, to have quality eye care for all of the people we know. Our eyes play such a huge factor in our lives so we want everyone to see the world the way they were meant to. In 1983 our founder Joyce White, was a devoted optmoetrist and decided to start her own buissness. With the love in her heart to help others her buissness grew all throughout London Ontario. The Spectacular Optometry buissness has been passed down through generations and continues the grow everyday. Today we have a very knowledgeable staff who are determined to get the job done right. Our leading optometrist Karen White studied at the University of Western Ontario, and specializes in all things eyes. Dr. White loves totravel and has been to many countries. In countire such as India and Bolivia she has dones lots of voulenteer work.
